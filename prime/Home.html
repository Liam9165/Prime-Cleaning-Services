A Bit About Us

Prime Cleaning Services is a family-owned and operated business. We established our company in 2016 with the aim of expanding and elevating the cleaning industry in Columbus. Every year, we strive to enhance our knowledge to better serve our valued clients.We offer a wide variety of residential and commercial cleaning services, including homes, offices, medical offices, commercial properties, Airbnbs, apartment turnovers, and more. We take great pride in our ability to tailor our services to meet the unique needs of each individual, ensuring customer satisfaction. With our exceptional customer support and highly skilled cleaning staff, we are confident that our services are unparalleled.
